The Simple Burger

Description

A delicious cheeseburger typically consists of a juicy beef patty cooked to perfection, topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, tangy pickles, and a savory sauce, all sandwiched between two fluffy buns. The combination of savory and slightly sweet flavors and contrasting textures make for a mouthwatering and satisfying burger experience.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Burger buns
  • Sauce

Steps

  • Preheat the grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  • Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  • Top each patty with a slice of cheese and let it melt for about 1-2 minutes.
  • To assemble the burger, spread sauce on the bottom bun, then add lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  • Place the patty with melted cheese on top of the veggies, then top with the other half of the bun.
  • Enjoy your delicious cheeseburger!
