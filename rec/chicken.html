The Simple Chicken Tenders

Description

Chicken tenders, also known as chicken strips or chicken fingers, are a popular type of food that consists of small pieces of chicken breast that have been breaded and fried until crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Chicken tenders are typically served as a main dish or appetizer and are often accompanied by dipping sauces such as barbecue sauce, honey mustard, or ranch dressing. The breading on chicken tenders can be made with a variety of ingredients, including breadcrumbs, flour, cornmeal, or panko, and can be seasoned with herbs and spices to add flavor. Chicken tenders are a versatile food that can be enjoyed in many different ways, whether as a snack, a side dish, or a meal. They are a popular option for both children and adults and can be found on the menus of many fast-food restaurants and casual dining establishments.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In another shallow dish, beat the eggs.
  4. In a third shallow dish, mix together the panko breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Dip each chicken strip first in the flour mixture, then in the beaten eggs, and finally in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing the breadcrumbs onto the chicken to ensure they adhere.
  6. Place the chicken strips on the prepared baking sheet and drizzle them with the vegetable oil.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the breadcrumbs are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove the chicken tenders from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving with your favorite dipping sauces.
  9. Enjoy your homemade chicken tenders!
