The Simple Milkshake

Description

A milkshake is a sweet, creamy beverage that is typically made with milk, ice cream, and flavorings such as syrup, fruit, or chocolate. To make a milkshake, the ingredients are blended together in a blender until they are smooth and thick. The consistency of a milkshake can vary depending on personal preference, with some people preferring a thicker shake while others prefer a more liquid consistency. Milkshakes can be made in a variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to more unique flavors such as strawberry, banana, or peanut butter. Some milkshakes are also made with added mix-ins such as candy, cookies, or fruit to add extra flavor and texture. Milkshakes are often served in tall glasses and topped with whipped cream and a cherry. They are a popular treat enjoyed by people of all ages, especially on hot summer days or as a sweet dessert after a meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend the ingredients on high speed until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  4. If desired, top the milkshake with whipped cream and a maraschino cherry.
  5. Enjoy your classic vanilla milkshake!
